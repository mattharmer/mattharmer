About

In the '90s, I elected computer programming in school. I used Basic on an Amstrad from home and was shown C+ and Java during school.

From 1996 to 2008, I worked in hospitality in Sydney, Brisbane and England. I earnt a few awards for bartending and performed well in a few competitions.

In 2008 I started building websites, mostly for small businesses. I wrote digital briefs, modified website templates for Joomla and Wordpress for around fifty sites hosted on a WHM/CPanel dedicated server.

10 years and learning.

HTML5 - CSS3 - Javascript

I owned a successful computer repair and sales store from 2012 for a few years. After closing, I travelled around Asia for 6 months.

Since 2015 I have been working in a few bar management positions and continuing work on Joomla and Wordpress sites.

I now manage around ten websites for friends and family while learning Javascript, API's, templating and other interesting code.
